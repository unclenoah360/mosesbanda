About Moses

Committed to Matero's Development

Moses Banda is a dedicated community leader, business consultant, and aspiring Member of Parliament for Matero Constituency. With a background in business and humanitarian work, he brings a practical, solution-oriented approach to politics.

As a BeRelevant Afrika Alumni and ambassador for multiple organizations, Moses has consistently demonstrated his commitment to citizen empowerment and community development. His approach is rooted in the belief that real change comes from taking initiative and acting with purpose.

Vision & Mission

Building a future where every citizen in Matero Constituency has access to opportunities, empowerment, and sustainable development.

My Vision

To transform Matero Constituency into a model of sustainable development, citizen empowerment, and community-led progress where every resident has access to opportunities and a voice in decision-making.

In Zambia, our development depends on citizens who rise with initiative, discipline, and purpose. Start now. Act boldly. Lead by example. That is how progress is born.

Through the Matero Constituency Development Forum, we aim to create a platform for genuine dialogue, unity, and development that puts the people of Matero at the center of all initiatives.

  • Economic empowerment for youth and women
  • Quality education and skills development
  • Improved healthcare access
  • Sustainable infrastructure development

My Mission

To serve as an accountable, transparent, and effective representative who bridges the gap between government and the people, ensuring that development reaches every corner of Matero Constituency.

  • Empower citizens socially, economically, and intellectually
  • Promote community-based development initiatives
  • Advocate for youth engagement and sports development
  • Foster business growth and entrepreneurship
  • Ensure transparent and accountable leadership
  • Bridge the gap between government services and citizens
  • Promote environmental sustainability

Development People's Party

Moses Banda is proudly affiliated with the Development People's Party (DPP), a fully registered political party committed to Zambia's transformation.

Official Party of Moses Banda

The Development People's Party (DPP) has achieved full registration status and is now fully empowered to undertake its mission to fundamentally reshape the political and economic destiny of Zambia.

DPP Ideology: Developmental Centrism

A balance of market-driven growth and state-led social investment.

Slogan: "Growth with a Heart: Development for All, Accountable to All."

Vision: The Zambian Century of Development

The DPP commits to building a Zambia where every citizen benefits from a diversified, strong, and morally governed economy. We reject the extremes of unsustainable spending and exclusionary market policies, choosing a path of Developmental Centrism where fiscal discipline funds infrastructure and human capital investment.

"The time for waiting is over; the time for work is now. We must immediately begin the concerted effort required to bring about the transformative change our nation has long awaited and truly deserves."

- Dr. Kafula Mubanga, DPP President

Life Philosophy & Lessons

Core principles and values that guide Moses Banda's approach to leadership and community service.

Respect as a Lifestyle

From 2017 to 2025, I learned that respect is not noise. It's quiet, consistent, and a lifestyle. Respect is attracted by discipline and earned through consistent, excellent conduct.

Empowering Citizens

One of the biggest reasons politics is over-emphasized in Zambia is widespread disempowerment. Empowered citizens question leaders, analyze decisions, and demand accountability.

Investing in People

The greatest investment on earth is not money; it is investing in people. When people feel respected and valued, they do their best.

Business & Family Legacy

Parents should register companies or start businesses that children can take over. Teach children skills and involve them in your work to create a foundation for their future.

Developmental Centrism

Rejecting extremes of unsustainable spending and exclusionary market policies in favor of a balanced approach that combines fiscal discipline with social investment.

Courage to Act

Real change starts when you decide to act, take responsibility, and step forward with courage. History is shaped by people who saw a problem and moved to solve it.

Time Management

Time control is the highest form of discipline. How you manage your time determines the quality of your life and effectiveness as a leader.

Spiritual Foundation

In Matthew 5, Jesus said, "We are the salt of the world." Our conduct, actions, and words must reflect that difference in all aspects of life and leadership.
